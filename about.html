Hello!

Hello! I am Xingjian Zhang, an undergraduate student at the University of Michigan and Shanghai Jiao Tong University, major in ECE and CS. My research interest is data mining, network and natural language processing. As an undergraduate student, I have been working on two research projects for semesters. I collaborated with Jiaqi Ma and Prof. Qiaozhu Mei on a data-mining project Fast Learning of MNL Model From General Partial Rankings with Application to Network Formation Modeling. It has been accepted by WSDM 2022 (acceptance rate ~20%). I am also working closely with Prof. Paramveer Dhillon on an ongoing NLP project, Debiasing Word Embeddings by Adversarial Training.

Additionally, I served as a teaching assistant of Honors Mathematics series courses at the UM-SJTU Joint Institute for semesters, advised by Prof. Horst Hohberger. I am also the assistant of the JI Alumni Association.
